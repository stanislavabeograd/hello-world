Keep track of your activities

It is important for you to keep track of your activities as PhD student, and this webpage should help you do so.

Please visit all of the subpages and enter your data. To keep your profile up to date, make sure you revisit this form once each semester. Having the data on all of your many activities as a PhD students will be helpful to export as a CV or apply for scholarships and grants.

Help other CIS students

By making your information available to other students in the program, you can support the information exchange and create new connections with your peers.

This form is specially designed so it will show your path through the CIS PhD program, thus others who are to make decisions on their path can come to you and ask you about your experiences, especially with other people, courses, exams and simiar.
